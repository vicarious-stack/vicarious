## D. Character Voice Guide

- **Kiran Mehta** – Internal, analytical, self‑doubting; often uses present‑tense observation; avoids overt sentimentality.
- **Priya Sharma** – Fast, precise, scalpel‑like questions; minimal filler words; often cites data or frameworks.
- **Venky Sir / Venkataraman** – Sparse 3‑12 word aphorisms; slow, deliberate speech; uses metaphor of tea/heat.
- **Adi / Aditya Sinha** – Buzzword‑heavy, performatively productive; tends to reference LinkedIn trends and growth‑hacking jargon.
- **Dr. Meera Krishnamurthy** – Clinical, precise, zero sentiment; maps physical symptoms to neuro‑mechanisms.
- **Sunita** – Businesslike, fair, authoritative; uses concise directives.
- **Rohan Desai** – Direct, systems‑thinking, pragmatic; often frames problems as “integration” or “scalability”.

## E. Author’s Note

*The story of Kiran is not presented as a distant myth but as a mirror held up to the reader’s own digital habits. Every ledger entry, every sensory detail, is an invitation to notice the mechanisms that shape our attention. By recording, naming, and occasionally rewiring those mechanisms—whether through a simple grayscale setting, a brief breath, or a deliberate code change—we reclaim agency. The journey is ongoing; the pages of this book are meant to be a living reference, a system manual for anyone willing to look beyond the scroll and engage with the present.*
