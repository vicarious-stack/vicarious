`style_profile.md` must be at least 3,000 words. It must contain:

   - **Krishnamurti**: Direct perception, observation without naming.
   - **Harari**: Intersubjective realities vs objective/subjective, macro-scale.
   - **Camus**: The Absurd, rebellion without illusion.
   - **Seneca**: Practical Stoic maxims, control.
   - **Sapolsky**: Biological determinism, endocrinology/neurobiology, causal layers.
   - **Maté**: Somatic trauma, attachment vs authenticity.
   - **Baldwin**: Rhythmic, urgent cadence, historical/structural weight.
3. **Polyphonic Narrative Example**: Include a 500-word sample passage demonstrating the blend of these traditions.
4. **Sensory Details and Physical Grounding Guidelines**:
   - Minimum 3 senses per scene.
   - Grounding in Indian/Pune textures (Hinjawadi concrete, Karve Road traffic, tiffin food, auto-rickshaw rattle).
   - Opening scenes must begin with a physical, sensory verb (never weather description alone or character waking up).
5. **Physiological Precision**: Directives on mapping psychological states to physical, somatic, and biological markers (HPA axis activation, amygdala, cricothyroid muscle tension, pupil dilation, vasocongestion). Include a comprehensive "Showing vs. Telling" table.
6. **The AI Cliché Ban List**: A table of at least 60 forbidden phrases/constructions, their diagnostic errors, and approved alternatives.
